🧪 Sterilization Parameters by Material Class

-   **Autoclaving (Steam Sterilization @ 121°C or 134°C):** This environment requires **3DXTECH ThermaX PEEK or PEKK**. Standard PLA, PLA+, and PCTG will melt or completely warp into an unusable shape inside a pressurized steam autoclave.
-   **Chemical Cold Washing (Isopropyl Alcohol or Hydrogen Peroxide Vapors):** This environment is ideal for **3D-Fuel Pro PCTG** or **3DXTECH PETG-CF**. These polymers feature excellent chemical resistance and will not degrade or craze when exposed to sanitizing washes. *Note: Avoid alcohol washes on raw PLA variants, as it can cause micro-brittleness over long exposure cycles.*
